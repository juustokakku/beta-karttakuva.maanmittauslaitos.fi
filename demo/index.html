General information

This page describes a BETA-service of Vector Tiles.

This BETA-service is developed and maintained by National Land Survey of Finland.
The service was developed in order to get a better understanding of how vector tiles work, and to get a better understanding of the possibilities and challenges related to the technology.

This page contains information about the Vector Tile service and some demo applications that uses the service. The demo applicatons are published in GitHub, so go ahead and test!

We look forward to hear you feedback. Please send us an mail on sovellustuki at maanmittauslaitos.fi / topic: Vector tiles
The aim is to develop the service further based on the received feedback.

Technical information

The vector tile service is published according to the WMTS-standard.

The metadata of the service is described in the Proof-of-Concept WMTS Capabilities document for the Vector Tiles Service used in demos.

The service contains the following themes as vector tiles layers: Buildings, Landuse, Roads, Water and Railroads from the Topographic Database of NLS Finland. The layers and their properties are named similar to Open Street Map.
In addition Cadastral parcels, Cadastral boundaries and Cadastral Boundary Markers are available.

Vector content is available on resolutions less than or equal to 16m. All raster and vector data is rendered and produced dynamically from databases.
Vector tile database is based on a snapshot from October 2017. The data and the vector tile service is for development use only and may change at any time.
The data in the service is Open data by National Land Survey of Finland and is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License .
